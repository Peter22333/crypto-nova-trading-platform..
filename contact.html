Frequently Asked Questions

How long do deposits take?

Crypto deposits are reviewed and credited within 1–24 hours after network confirmation. Bank transfers take 1–3 business days depending on your bank.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email address immediately.

How long do withdrawals take?

Crypto withdrawals are processed within 24 hours pending admin review. Bank withdrawals take 2–5 business days.

What documents are required for verification?

A government-issued photo ID (passport or national ID) and proof of address (utility bill or bank statement) dated within 3 months.
