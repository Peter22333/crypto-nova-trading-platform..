Frequently Asked Questions

Can I withdraw my funds at any time?

Yes. There is no lock-up period for the Conservative or Balanced portfolios. The Aggressive and Crypto portfolios have a 7-day notice period to allow orderly position liquidation.

How are management fees charged?

We charge a 1.5% annual management fee deducted monthly from the portfolio value. There are no performance fees. All costs are fully disclosed in advance.

How often is performance reported?

Daily performance updates are shown in your dashboard. Monthly reports with full attribution analysis are emailed to all portfolio investors.
