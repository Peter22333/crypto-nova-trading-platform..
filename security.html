How We Protect Your Assets

Fund Segregation

Client funds are held in segregated accounts, completely separate from Crypto Nova operating funds. This means your money cannot be used for any corporate purpose and is always available for withdrawal.

Multi-Signature Wallets

All crypto withdrawals require multiple cryptographic signatures from geographically distributed key holders. No single person or system can authorise a withdrawal alone — eliminating insider risk entirely.

Real-Time Monitoring

Our 24/7 security operations centre monitors for anomalous activity across all accounts and systems. Suspicious logins, unusual withdrawal patterns, and API anomalies trigger immediate automated lockdowns pending human review.

Data Privacy

We collect only the minimum personal data required to operate and comply with regulations. Your data is never sold to third parties. All data stored in EU-compliant infrastructure with full GDPR adherence.

Bug Bounty Program

Security researchers who responsibly disclose vulnerabilities are rewarded through our bug bounty programme. We value the global security community as a partner in protecting our users.
