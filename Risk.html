Risk Disclosure Statement

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Please read this risk disclosure carefully before investing or trading on our platform.

⚠️ Important Warning

Trading in cryptocurrencies, forex, and leveraged products involves substantial risk and may not be suitable for all investors. You may lose some or all of your invested capital. Do not invest money you cannot afford to lose.

1. General Investment Risk

All investments carry risk. The value of investments and the income from them can fall as well as rise. Past performance of any asset or investment strategy is not a reliable indicator of future performance. Projected returns are estimates only and are not guaranteed.

2. Cryptocurrency Risk

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile and largely unregulated. Prices can fluctuate dramatically within short periods. Additional risks include:

  • Extreme price volatility — assets can lose significant value rapidly
  • Regulatory risk — government actions may affect cryptocurrency values
  • Technology risk — blockchain networks can experience failures or forks
  • Liquidity risk — some assets may be difficult to sell at desired prices
  • Custody risk — loss of private keys may result in permanent loss of funds
  • Counterparty risk — exchanges and counterparties may become insolvent

3. Forex and Leverage Risk

Forex and leveraged trading amplifies both potential gains and potential losses. Trading on margin means you can lose more than your initial deposit. A small adverse market movement can result in significant losses relative to your invested capital. Leverage of 1:500 means a 0.2% adverse price move wipes out your entire margin.

4. Managed Investment Plan Risk

Our managed investment plans use algorithmic and manual trading strategies with projected returns. These projections are based on historical performance and modelling — they are not guarantees. Actual returns may differ materially from projections. You may receive less than you invested.

5. Cloud Mining Risk

Cloud mining contracts are subject to cryptocurrency price risk, network difficulty changes, and operational risks. Revenue from mining is not guaranteed and may be lower than projected. Mining contracts are not principal-protected investments.

6. Liquidity Risk

You may not always be able to liquidate positions or withdraw funds immediately, particularly during periods of market stress. Withdrawal processing times are subject to platform review and may be extended during unusual market conditions or compliance reviews.

7. Operational and Cybersecurity Risk

Despite our security measures, no system is immune to attack. System outages, cyberattacks, or technical failures could affect your ability to access or manage your account. We maintain insurance and security reserves but cannot guarantee complete protection against all risks.

8. Regulatory Risk

The regulatory environment for crypto assets and online trading continues to evolve globally. Changes in regulation could affect the availability of services, returns, or the regulatory status of assets held on our platform.

9. Seek Independent Advice

We strongly recommend that before investing, you seek independent financial, legal, and tax advice tailored to your personal circumstances. This disclosure does not constitute financial advice. Crypto Nova is not liable for investment decisions made based on information provided on the platform.

10. Acknowledgement

By using Crypto Nova's services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted the risks described in this disclosure. You confirm that you are investing with funds you can afford to lose and that you are making investment decisions of your own free will.
