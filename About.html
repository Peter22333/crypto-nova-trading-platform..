Who We Are

Crypto Nova is a globally regulated financial technology company founded in 2018 with a singular mission: to democratise access to institutional-grade trading infrastructure for individual investors worldwide.

We started with a small team of ex-Goldman Sachs and JP Morgan engineers who believed that retail traders deserved the same execution speed, analytical tools, and asset access previously reserved for hedge funds and banks. Today, over 1.2 million investors across 94 countries trust Crypto Nova to grow and protect their capital.
